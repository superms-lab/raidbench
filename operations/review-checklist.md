# Content Review Checklist

Use this before publishing generated guide pages.

## Required Checks

- The page answers one concrete player question.
- The title matches a real search/problem phrase.
- Any game numbers are either from RaidBench data or marked as requiring in-game verification.
- The page links to the calculator or a relevant next guide.
- The page links naturally to the paid pack only when the pack solves the next step.
- No copied community text is used as final prose.
- No "official" wording is used for RaidBench.
- The page includes a last-checked date or source note.
- Mobile layout has no horizontal overflow.

## Reject Or Rewrite If

- The page is a generic beginner article.
- The page depends on unstable patch-specific claims without a source note.
- The page only repeats another guide without a table, checklist, or example.
- The paid CTA feels unrelated to the problem.
- The page encourages spam promotion or misleading claims.

## Fast Publish Rule

Generated pages can be published after a 3-minute review if:

- source notes are present
- numbers are conservative
- title/problem/short answer/checklist are coherent
- QA passes
