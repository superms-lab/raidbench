# Solo Raid Checklist

Before leaving base:

- Calculate visible raid path.
- Bring enough boom for one extra layer.
- Bring building plan and seal materials.
- Bring meds, ammo, fallback weapon, and armor you can afford to lose.
- Decide escape route.
- Decide stop condition.

During raid:

- Bank early wins.
- Do not keep booming after the target becomes bad value.
- Leave if counters control your exit.
