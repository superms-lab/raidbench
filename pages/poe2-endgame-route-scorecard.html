POE2 endgame route scorecard

A POE2 endgame route scorecard for comparing farming, bossing, mapping, and progression choices.

View audit credits Back to POE2 Lab

Short answer

Choose endgame content by fit, not hype. Score each route by build compatibility, safety, investment cost, learning burden, trade friction, and whether it moves your next upgrade forward.

Score areaLow scoreHigh score
Build fitYour build struggles with the mechanic.Your build clears it naturally.
SafetyFrequent deaths or failed runs.Stable completion with low deaths.
InvestmentRequires gear or entry cost you cannot sustain.Can start with current resources.
LearningYou do not understand why runs fail.Mistakes are clear and fixable.
Upgrade pathRewards do not help your next goal.Outputs directly fund or support upgrades.

Checklist

  • List two possible routes and score both before switching.
  • Run a small test batch instead of changing your whole plan.
  • Track completion rate before profit claims.
  • Do not run content your build barely survives.
  • Pick the route that funds the next upgrade, not the most popular route.

Example

A route can be worse for a top player but better for you if it has fewer deaths, less setup cost, and clearer upgrades.

Common mistakes

  • Switching route every time a video claims a new best strategy.
  • Ignoring failure rate.
  • Counting rewards that you cannot sell or use.
  • Choosing bossing when the build is still a mapping build.
