POE2 currency farming checklist

A POE2 currency farming checklist for choosing repeatable content, avoiding over-investment, and tracking profit honestly.

View audit credits Back to POE2 Lab

Short answer

The best farming route is the one your build can repeat quickly and safely. Before copying a high-end strategy, check entry cost, death risk, clear speed, trade effort, and whether your build can handle the content consistently.

Checklist

  • Choose one repeatable route before changing your whole build.
  • Track ten runs before judging profit.
  • Count deaths, entry cost, and unsold items.
  • Prefer stable farming over risky high-roll claims.
  • Upgrade only the bottleneck that slows the route.

Example

A flashy route that needs expensive setup may be worse than a safer route your build can repeat with low deaths and low trade friction.

Common mistakes

  • Calling a route profitable based on one lucky drop.
  • Ignoring entry cost and failed runs.
  • Copying a strategy from a build with much better clear speed.
  • Counting items at listed value instead of sold value.
